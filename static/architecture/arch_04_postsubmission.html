Architecture · Section 04

Post-Submission Pipeline

After a claim is submitted, three agents run to evaluate and route it — Compliance and Fraud execute in parallel via LangGraph fan-out, then the Advisor synthesises all findings into a routing decision with email notification and a full audit trail.

Verdict Matrix

All possible verdicts and what triggers them

VerdictSourceTrigger conditionOutcome
pass Compliance No violations; within all policy caps Advisor may auto-approve
requiresManagerApproval Compliance Soft violation (≤150% cap) + coherent justification Advisor escalates to reviewer
requiresDirectorApproval Compliance / B5 Soft-plus violation (>150% cap) or B5 hard-cap exceeded Advisor escalates — director must approve
requiresReview B6 critique / parse fail Self-critique disagrees with primary verdict OR LLM output unparseable Advisor always escalates
fail Compliance Hard violation (alcohol, personal, non-claimable) Advisor returns to claimant
duplicate_fraud Fraud Exact duplicate in DB (same merchant, amount, date, employee) Advisor escalates with fraud flag
suspicious Fraud LLM detects statistical anomaly (high frequency, unusual amount) Advisor escalates for review
