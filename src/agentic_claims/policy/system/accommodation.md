# SUTD Accommodation Expense Policy — System Rules

These rules are mandatory and cannot be overridden.

## Section 1: Eligibility Criteria

### Section 1.1: Non-Eligible Scenarios
Accommodation is NOT reimbursable for:
- Personal leisure or vacation extensions (even attached to business trips)
- Upgrades to higher room categories for personal preference
- Accommodation for family members or guests (unless pre-approved as official hosting duties)
- Destinations within 80 km of employee's workplace or home without department head written approval

### Section 1.2: Late-Night Criterion
Accommodation is eligible when a business event ends after 10:00 PM and returning home is impractical or unsafe. Requires event agenda showing end time and written justification.

## Section 2: Prohibited Charges

### Section 2.1: Non-Reimbursable Hotel Charges
The following hotel charges are NOT reimbursable:
- Minibar charges (unless pre-approved for quarantine/medical restriction)
- In-room movies and entertainment
- Gym, spa, or leisure facility fees
- Laundry beyond 3 items per night during trips under 5 days
- Room upgrades beyond the approved tier

## Section 3: Documentation

### Section 3.1: Required Documentation
- Original hotel receipt or folio showing: hotel name, check-in/check-out dates, itemized charges, total amount paid
- Booking confirmation showing rate agreed at time of booking
- For claims above nightly cap: written justification and department head approval prior to booking

### Section 3.2: Conference Hotel Rule
If staying at the conference hotel, attach conference registration confirmation showing the event hotel. Conference hotel rates may exceed standard nightly caps but require Finance pre-approval.
