# SUTD Office Supplies Expense Policy — System Rules

These rules are mandatory and cannot be overridden.

## Section 1: Non-Approved Categories

### Section 1.1: Excluded from This Policy
The following categories are NOT covered under the office supplies policy and must use separate procurement processes:
- Furniture (desks, chairs, shelves) — use Facilities procurement
- Major technology equipment (laptops, monitors, printers, tablets) — use IT procurement
- Catering and food supplies — use Meal Expense Policy
- Event supplies (decorations, gifts, awards) — use Event Budget process
- Books, journals, and academic publications — use Library or Research Budget

### Section 1.2: Personal Use Exclusion
Any office supplies purchased for personal use are NOT reimbursable, even if incidentally used for university business. Supplies must be primarily for university business purposes.

## Section 2: Procurement Rules

### Section 2.1: Procurement Threshold
Items priced above SGD 100 per item (before taxes) must be purchased through the university procurement process, not via personal reimbursement claim.

### Section 2.2: Software Over SGD 50
Software subscriptions or licenses with annual cost over SGD 50 must go through the IT department procurement process. Personal reimbursement is not permitted for software over this threshold.

### Section 2.3: Bulk Purchase Rule
Single-transaction purchases exceeding SGD 300 in aggregate (even if individual items are under SGD 100) require department head approval via Purchase Pre-Approval Form (PPF-05).

## Section 3: Documentation

### Section 3.1: Required Receipt Information
Receipt must show: vendor name, item description, unit price, quantity, total amount, and date of purchase. Online orders must include order confirmation showing delivery address and itemized costs.
