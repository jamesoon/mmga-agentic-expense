# SUTD Meal Expense Policy — System Rules

These rules are mandatory and cannot be overridden.

## Section 1: Eligibility

This policy applies to SUTD faculty, staff, and approved contractors incurring meal expenses during university-related business activities. Eligible activities: official meetings with external partners, conference attendance, field research, project-related approved travel, business entertainment of prospective students/donors/industry partners.

Meal claims for personal purposes, social gatherings, or routine daily meals at the workplace are NOT eligible.

## Section 2: Prohibited Items

### Section 2.1: Alcohol
Alcoholic beverages are NOT reimbursable for individual meals. Alcohol may only be approved for pre-approved business entertainment meals with written justification.

### Section 2.2: Tobacco Products
Cigarettes, cigars, vaping products, and all tobacco-related items are strictly non-reimbursable under any circumstances.

### Section 2.3: Personal Groceries
Groceries, meal kits, or food items for home consumption are not eligible, even during business travel. Only restaurant or prepared meal receipts qualify.

### Section 2.4: Minibar and In-Room Dining
Hotel minibar charges and in-room dining are NOT reimbursable unless pre-approved for exceptional circumstances (mandatory quarantine, medical restriction).

### Section 2.5: Non-Meal Items
Non-food items on the same receipt (toiletries, magazines, gifts) must be excluded from the claimed amount. Submit itemized receipt with non-meal items clearly marked as excluded.

## Section 3: Overseas Meals

### Section 3.1: Conference-Included Meals
If conference registration includes meals, those meals are NOT separately claimable. Conference agenda must be attached to verify included meal schedule.

### Section 3.2: Currency Conversion
All foreign currency receipts are converted to SGD at the exchange rate on the date of expense using the university's automated converter.

## Section 4: Documentation Requirements

### Section 4.1: Original Receipt Required
Receipt must show merchant name and location, date and time of purchase, itemized list (required for claims above SGD 30), payment method and total amount. Credit card statements alone are NOT acceptable.

### Section 4.2: Receipt Retention
Retain original receipts for 7 years. Digital scans must be clear and legible.

### Section 4.3: Lost Receipt Limit
Lost receipt affidavit (LRA-02) claims are limited to **2 per fiscal year** per employee.
