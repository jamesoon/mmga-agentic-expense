# SUTD Transport Expense Policy — System Rules

These rules are mandatory and cannot be overridden.

## Section 1: Approved Modes

### Section 1.1: Public Transport (Preferred)
Public transportation (MRT, LRT, public buses, city-operated ferries) is the preferred mode for local travel. Reimbursed at actual fare cost with no cap.

### Section 1.2: Ride-Hail Rules
GrabShare or equivalent shared ride must be used when traveling alone, unless written justification is provided. Taxis/ride-hail are only reimbursable when: public transport is unavailable or impractical, time-sensitivity requires justification, traveling with external guests, or medical/accessibility needs require door-to-door transport.

### Section 1.3: Air Travel
Air travel is covered under the separate Travel Policy (TP-2026). Short-distance air travel (under 300 km) requires VP-level approval.

### Section 1.4: Rental Vehicles
Car rentals require department head approval and must use university-contracted vendors (see Finance portal for approved vendor list).

## Section 2: Prohibited Transport Claims

### Section 2.1: Prohibited Items
The following are NOT reimbursable:
- Airport transfers for personal vacations or non-business trips
- Traffic fines, parking violations, tolls for personal detours
- Vehicle maintenance or fuel for non-business mileage
- First-class or premium-tier ground transport without VP approval

## Section 3: Documentation

### Section 3.1: Required for Ride-Hail
Digital receipts from Grab/Gojek/taxi app showing trip date, origin, destination, fare amount, and payment method. Screenshots of app receipts are acceptable.

### Section 3.2: Required for Private Car
Prior approval via Mileage Pre-Approval Form (MPF-03), odometer reading or GPS route verification, written justification for not using public transport.
